About This Blog

This blog is dedicated to shedding light on mental health challenges faced by Kenyan youths. It aims to provide awareness, share personal stories, and offer practical guidance to those struggling or seeking to support others.

Key Mental Health Challenges

Rising Levels of Anxiety and Depression

Recent years have seen an increase in anxiety and depression cases among Kenyan youths due to academic pressure, unemployment, and societal expectations.

Stigma and Lack of Awareness

Many young people hesitate to seek help due to fear of judgment, cultural beliefs, or lack of knowledge about mental health services.

Limited Access to Mental Health Services

Access to affordable and quality mental health care remains a challenge, particularly in rural areas.
